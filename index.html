Game rules - The game has two players playing in rounds - With each turn a player rolls a dice as many time he wishes - Each result gets added to his (current) round score - If the player rolls a 1, all round score gets lost - After that it's the next player's turn - The player can choose hold, so his round score gets added to his global score - After that it's again the next player's turn - The first player to reach 50 points wins the game - Don't be nervous
