Game rules - The game has two players playing in rounds - With each turn a player rolls the dice as many time he wishes - Each result gets added to his round score - If a player rolls a 1 two times in a row, all round score gets lost - If a player rolls a 6 two times in a row he looses his entire score - After that it's the next player's turn - A player can choose hold, so his round score gets added to his global score - After that it's again the next player's turn - If a player's score is equal or exceeds the amount of points in the set limit box press hold to win the game. Pressing roll dice the game continues - Before start playing set a limit in the set limit box - Have fun
